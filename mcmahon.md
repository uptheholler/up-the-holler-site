---
title: McMahon Creek
subtitle: 28.1 miles, 91 square miles of Belmont County
order: 3
---

From the headwaters in Union Township, southeast and then east to the Ohio
River at Bellaire. Coal was mined along it as early as 1828.

Ohio EPA studied the basin in 2009 and published in 2010; a new study of
Captina Creek, McMahon Creek and selected Ohio River tributaries is underway.
USGS maintains gages at Glencoe and at Bellaire.
